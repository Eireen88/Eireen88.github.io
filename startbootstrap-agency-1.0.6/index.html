About Instructional Patterns

Definition

Pattern demontrates how teachers can facilitate the interactivity of these patterns to match their goals for student learning. these interactive patterns are reinforced through the incorporation of medial, cognitive and behavioral neuroscence research. Patterns give teachers new ideas and ways to shape their lessons. Special attention is given to the inclusion of digital media to the teaching lessons.

Use/h4>

This website helps teachers to choose suitable Instructional Patterns for their (teaching) lessons. E.g. to make them more varied.

About the Project

Background-Story

Results emerging from multiple research projects have been showing promising possibilities for the inclusion of ICT in schools. However, the actual adoption of ICT in schools is much less successful. The project’s partners are departments from University Education of Weingarten (Germany), the Open University of Cyprus (Cyprus) and Linnaeus University (Sweden).
